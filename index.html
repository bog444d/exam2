Not only people need a house

Our shelter helps pets find new homes. We love the pets we shelter and believe that every cat or dog deserves a loving family.

About the shelter "Cozy House"

Currently we are taking care of about 60 cats and 30 dogs. All our pets are spayed and vaccinated. We have been operating since 2010 and have helped over 1000 pets find their new families. We strive to make the lives of our pets happier and provide them with all necessary treatments, vaccinations, and training.
